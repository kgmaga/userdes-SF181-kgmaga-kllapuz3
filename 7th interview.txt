Interviewee: Rhyss Cadungan
Age: 19

What are your thoughts on the allotted time for lunch breaks, is it enough? 
- It is more than enough. Aside from eating your lunch, you can still do a lot of stuff with that given time. 

How do you spend your breaks? 
- Eating together with friends and doing school related stuff.  

Are the food choices on the cafeteria enough? - Yes, the food stalls have different set of menu. 

How about the food line queuing? 
- Some students sometimes cut in line

What are the reasons for you not to eat on the cafeteria and where do you usually eat out?
- The cafeteria is usually crowded especially on our breaktime and food is a bit pricey. We usually eat at Mang Danny's Eatery
